HOW TO PUT THESE FILES ON GITHUB
================================

After you unzip this, you will see THREE things:

    index.html      (a file)
    assets          (a folder)
    modules         (a folder)

Do this:

1. Go to your repository on GitHub.
2. Click "Add file", then "Upload files".
3. OPEN the unzipped folder so you can see the three things above.
4. Select ALL THREE (index.html, assets, modules) and drag them
   into the upload box.

   IMPORTANT: drag the three things INSIDE the folder.
   Do NOT drag the whole outer folder itself. That is the mistake
   that nested everything one level too deep last time.

5. Scroll down and click "Commit changes".
6. Wait about a minute, then open your live site and click a
   module card. It should open the module page, and the page
   should be styled (indigo bar at the top, gold line under it).

That's it. This upload fixes everything in one commit.

OPTIONAL TIDY-UP (the site works fine without this):
Your repo has two leftover items from earlier that do nothing:
a file called "styles.css" at the top level, and a folder called
"course-site-pages". You can delete them anytime. Nothing links
to them, so leaving them does no harm.
